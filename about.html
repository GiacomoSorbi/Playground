About Veruscript

Together we can make a difference in the world of academia.

Now is a new era of access to scientific knowledge. Transformed and enriched by technology, researchers now have a groundbreaking opportunity to exchange knowledge which can further promote scientific research. Our mission as start-up is simple: to rethink how scholarly publishing can advance this global exchange of information.

United with the needs of researchers, our goal is to make publication efficient, transparent, accessible and fair.

As all researchers know, there are different stages in the development of new ideas, sharing those ideas is what helps to bring the significant change. Our intention is to enhance communication between researchers, scientists and institutions by giving them a place to come together and discuss current scientific developments.

Our aim is to create a reliable and resourceful publishing platform which will inspire further dissemination and exchange of knowledge concerning the exploration and development of science for the public benefit. The Veruscript platform provides excellent opportunities for researchers which enable strengthening professional competence and generating new knowledge. Knowledge is the key to the future.

The Veruscript platform is intended to bring together specialists across all fields of STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Medicine) and HSS (Humanities and Social Sciences).

Veruscript represents a community that understands the need for a different approach in publishing process. Our new model empowers authors with choices that help them achieve the best outcome for their manuscripts. Our model also rewards peer reviewers, ensuring that valuable resources stay within the research community.

VERUSCRIPT SUPPORTS THE ENTIRE RESEARCH COMMUNITY

Veruscript represents a community that understands the need for a different approach in publishing process. Our new model empowers authors with choices that help them achieve the best outcome for their manuscripts. Our model also rewards peer reviewers, ensuring that valuable resources stay within the research community.

THE VERUSCRIPT PUBLISHING PROCESS

Bespoke, easy-to-use manuscript management platform. The Editor in Chief of each journal can choose between Gold Open Access or Fair Access model. Authors are invited to submit any form of scholarly communication.

  • 1 The Author can choose the relevant option:
    • Double-blind or open peer-review
    • Payment of APC upon submission or upon acceptance
  • 2 At submission stage:
    • Manuscript is triaged for scientific integrity and suitability
    • Authors are verified for authenticity
    • Text is checked for plagiarism
  • 3 The appointed Editor identifies reviewers. Manuscript is sent to independent experts for review. Reviewers receive compensation or credit upon completion.
  • 4 The independent Editor assesses peer review comments and submits his decision which is based on quality of research.
  • 5 At acceptance stage author can choose to publish manuscript in relevant Veruscript journal or to withdraw his paper to submit to any other journal. If rejected, the author is provided with constructive review comments.
